Our Partners
At Refrigeration Solutions, we collaborate with trusted industry leaders to deliver high-quality equipment and reliable solutions. Our partners share our commitment to excellence, ensuring every project benefits from cutting-edge technology and professional expertise.
Docriluc
Docriluc is a Spanish company specialized in the design and manufacture of commercial and industrial refrigeration for hotels and restaurants. It has eight flexible production chains, equipped with the most advanced techniques, which allow us to offer an agile response and certified quality, contrasted with the implementation of ISO 9001:2015. It is also provided with an environmental management system that optimizes natural resources, managing waste and its recycling controlled by ISO 14001:2015.
UBC Group
UBC Group is a global leader in B2B distribution, providing innovative, customer-focused solutions across refrigeration, promotional products, engineering, and service. With divisions like UBC Cool, UBC Promo, and UBC Service, they manufacture world-class equipment, deliver rapid support, and ensure top-quality products reach customers efficiently. Their mission is to anticipate customer needs, combining expertise, innovation, and passion to lead markets worldwide. From professional refrigeration fridges to microbrewery solutions, UBC Group exemplifies excellence, reliability, and creativity, making them a trusted partner for Refrigeration Solutions and a driving force in delivering tailored, high-quality solutions to businesses globally.
Serrco
With over 30 years of experience in professional beverage refrigeration, Serrco is a trusted name in the hospitality industry, both in the Netherlands and internationally.

They continuously develop new products in close collaboration with customers, emphasizing reliability, durability, and high-quality production. Focused on efficiency and cost control, Serrco delivers competitive products while striving for maximum customer satisfaction, from pricing and delivery to on-site service. Their flexibility and dedication make them a full-fledged partner for the international beverage industry. For more information or specific requests, Serrco welcomes direct contact.
Lancer
Established in 1967, Lancer began by manufacturing parts for beverage dispensing machines and has since grown into a leading provider of complete beverage dispensing solutions.

Serving restaurants, convenience stores, cinemas, hotels, theme parks, stadiums, and more, they combine decades of experience with high-quality manufacturing. Headquartered in San Antonio, Texas, with facilities in San Antonio and Piedras Negras, Mexico, they deliver reliable, efficient solutions tailored to the needs of businesses worldwide, making them a trusted and innovative partner in the beverage industry.
