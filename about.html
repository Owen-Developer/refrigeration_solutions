About Us
Refrigeration Solutions has its origins stretching to 1954 when Tom McDonald opened his Refrigeration company in Kilkenny , Ireland--a lot of changes in the Industry since then!

Offering a range of services for Refrigeration in the Hospitality, Food and related Industries , Refrigeration Solutions will ensure that you get the best advice matched with proper design, whether it's a start up or existing concern. Focusing carefully on the design side, we match the Refrigeration needs with equipment and systems , particularly focussing on the energy side of things. We can project manage and oversee from start to finish. With Refrigeration manufacturing partners worldwide, we can keep costs on hardware to a minimum whilst ensuring quality and sustainability Having worked closely with ALL Hospitality Associations and Breweries in UK and Ireland over the years , we feel that our value to the customer is like no other in the industry.
Areas Of Expertise
Hotels, bars, restaurants.
There are huge issues around Refrigeration in these sectors , mainly on sustainability , and we have various solutions. Refrigeration , by its nature , needs close design attention and we can create solutions for new builds and existing businesses.. Working with the Irish Hotel Federation , UK Hospitality , Licensed Vintners Association and Vintners Federation over many years , our LANCER Glycol system ( video attached ) has proven itself to be a game changer . Along with the Irish and UK Breweries , this system has proven itself to one and all. Also we design a DIGITAL compressor system which can operate in large and small establishments also, cutting energy costs by approx 30-40% We then recover the heat from the Refrigeration to give FREE hot water to the premises.

On the hardware side such as Kitchen/Bar/Food Refrigeration , we offer a direct line to our manufacturing partners , thus cutting the middleman out and ensuring relly good capitla costs for quality equipment.

Our Associations with all leading manufacturers and industry experts inc Breweries enables us to us design the proper equipment for specific needs: Food Manufacturing , Processing Factories etc.
